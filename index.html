...I've never lived so close to a body of water. The lake is very interesting to me... it is so big. Looking out at it feels like looking at the ocean. 

There are people here, and it is irritating. 





The water isnt as blue as I thought it would be. Its green and hazy and deep and cold. I want to touch it, but it smells like skin and mold and people. 

I dipped my boot in the water, and now my socks are just wet. No, theyre not wet I can just feel the coldness of the water through the leather.






...I feel guilty, wearing leather because it is the skin of an animal that could think and feel and breathe. An animal that probably felt love and worried about its family. 


...I can feel the sun on my skin and that makes me worry.







Its getting hot in the sun. My jacket is black and leather, but I dont wanna take it off because it smells like the friend that I let borrow it . I feel gross for admitting this, but I like feeling like they're there”it makes me feel less alone.


...there are two dead leaves, floating in the water. Side-by-side. 






Im sitting on cold, hard concrete that a human being took the time to make.

Im glad that I have my jacket even if its making me feel weird. Why should I be ashamed? I wish I could jump into the water and sink to the bottom and lie there until the end of time.


I took a photo of my shadow to prove that I was really here. 









The lake is making me feel nervous. It feels massive and overbearing now. I feel sick. I always feel sick. I smell bacteria and someone elses sweat on my clothes. I wonder if he thinks about me. The lake probably feel sick. It holds the memories of so many bodies. Id feel sick if I were the lake. It behaves so calmly for the amount of trauma it has endured. I cant say Im not jealous.






The sun is shining hot on my skin again, but I dont want to take off my jacket. Im scared of being alone.


The water looks like skin. It moves with a human softness. I want to brush it with my hand.
